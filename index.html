Our Story: Built on a Dream, Proven by Results

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate educators in the heart of Jodhpur, Takshashila Coaching Center was born from a simple but powerful belief: Every student in Rajasthan deserves access to world-class coaching for competitive exams.

Named after the ancient Takshashila University — the world's first university and a symbol of knowledge and excellence — our institute carries forward that legacy of rigorous, focused, and personalized education.

Over the past 15 years, we have guided 1,200+ students to their dream careers in engineering and medicine through our unwavering commitment to offline classroom coaching — because we believe that nothing replaces the energy, discipline, and mentorship of a real classroom environment.

We are not a platform. We are not an app. We are teachers who care, who know every student by name, and who celebrate every rank as our own achievement.

Our Infrastructure

Built for learning, designed for focus — our facility gives students everything they need to succeed.

Spacious Classrooms

Modern, well-ventilated classrooms with maximum 35 students per batch. Green boards, projector screens, and optimal seating for focused learning.

Dedicated Study Rooms

Silent study rooms available from 7 AM to 8 PM for enrolled students. The perfect environment for self-study between classes.

Doubt Clearing Sessions

Dedicated 1-hour doubt sessions every day with subject faculty. Every concept is cleared before moving forward — no student is left behind.

Test Series Hall

Dedicated hall for weekly and monthly tests in exam-like conditions. Regular mock tests build speed, accuracy, and confidence.

Study Material Library

Extensive collection of JEE, NEET, and Foundation material including previous year papers, reference books, and our proprietary modules.

Student Counselling Cell

Dedicated counsellors for academic and psychological support. We guide students through exam stress, college admissions, and career planning.
